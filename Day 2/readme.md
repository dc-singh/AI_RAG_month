## 1. 4 Prompt Patters


- Role Assigment - It tells ai exactly who it is. The more specific the role -> betterr the answer.

- OutPut format control - Tells ai Exactly how to formate the response

- Few-Shot Prompting - Ai Learns the patterns form examples then applies it to new input.

- Chain of thought - Tells AI to think step by step, AI show its reasoning, Results are more accurate.
